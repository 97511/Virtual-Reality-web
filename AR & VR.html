The diffrence between AR & VR..

VR (Virtual Reality)
a computer-generated environment with scenes and objects that appear to be real, making the user feel they are immersed in their surroundings.
AR (Augmented Reality)
technology that superimposes digital information onto the real world, creating an enhanced experience for the user.
